3. How We Use Your Information

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

Each platform has its own privacy policy governing the data they share with us.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure token storage (localStorage with encryption)
  • No server-side storage of sensitive credentials

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke: Disconnect third-party integrations at any time
  • Opt-out: Stop using the service

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies and localStorage for:

  • Authentication tokens
  • User preferences
  • Session management

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the Service.
